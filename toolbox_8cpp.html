Use the texture_coordinates (u,v) from a pointcloud to recover pixel (x,y) coordinates in the texture frame and get its RGB values. We multiply the texture_coordinates with the texture image's resolution (and clamp it) to get the (x,y) pixel from the texture image where we can query its RGB values.

Collect the color and depth frame from the camera and start the stream. Depending on the publishers_on_off arguments passed in the function, different stream will be published, among the following : color, depth, filtered_depth, colorized_depth, colorized_filtered_depth, raw_pointcloud and fitlered_poincloud. Note : if the filtered depth isn't pusblished, neither can be the filtered_colorized depth and the filtered pointcloud The colorized depths (filtered are not) are created with the colorize filter, while the pointclouds can be colorized by mapping them with the color_frame, the dept_frame (gray scale) or a colorized depth_frame.
